instructions

step 1

In a gallon size reseable bag, combine lime juice, crushed garlicc, orange juice cilantro, salt, pepper. vegetable oil, jalano, and vinegar, Squeeze it around to nix it up.

step 2

Put the entire flank steak into the reseable bag. Seal it up tight. Make sure all the meat is exposed to the marinada, squishing the bag around to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2hrs, or overnight.

step 3

Heat an outdoor grill to high heat

step 4

Remove the flank steak from the marinade, and discard excess marinade. Cook on grill for 7 to 10 minutes per side,

step 5

Once done, remove from heat and let rest 10 minutes. Slice then and serve.
